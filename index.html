About Me

My name is William Chandler, and I'm training to become a full-stack web developer through UT's Coding Bootcamp. I'm currently expanding my knowledge of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Also in the docket are Node.js, MongoDB, React.js, Laravel, and a flurry of others.

I have degrees in Spanish and International Relations, and have worked in study abroad and international human rights. But for some time I've been interested in programming and web development. I decided to take the plunge, and I'm confident I made the right choice. I enjoy learning, overcoming challenges, and creating.

A native Texan, I've lived in Mexico, Scotland, and Spain. My family [partner: Jacqueline; son: Lochlan; dogs: Maggie + Griffin] and I are currently based in Austin, with dreams of the Pacific Northwest.

Please take a look at my portfolio and get in touch if I can help turn your concepts into sharp, efficient web realities. I hope to work with you soon!
